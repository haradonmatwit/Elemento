TUTORIAL

Avoid as many obstacles as you can to get through the ruins, but be very careful or else you will have to start over!

ARROW KEYS

Use the arrow buttons to navigate each level and reach the door to the next level!

Left Arrow Key

Move Left

Right Arrow Key

Move Right

Up Arrow Key

Jump Up

Down Arrow Key

Enter Door

LETTER KEYS

Switch between the four elemental states unlocked individually along the way to navigate obstacles!

W Key

Switch to Water

A Key

Switch to Air

S Key

Switch to Fire

D Key

Switch to Earth

ELEMENTAL OBSTACLES

There are numerous elemental obstacles within each level, use the power of the elements accordingly to overcome them!

Pass fire pits by extinguishing them with water

Cross water pits by freezing the water with air

Clear ice blocks on the path by melting them with fire

REGULAR OBSTACLES

Besides elemental obstacles, there are also regular obstacles!

There are pits full of spikes, avoid them at all costs by jumping over them!
