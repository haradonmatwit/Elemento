ABOUT ELEMENTO

This game is for a Web Development Final Project in which Team STRM members Randy, Meagan, Tiffany and Sabrina created a game based on a version of Fireboy and Watergirl they all played when they were younger and enjoyed so much as kids. This game was developed as they reminisced their youth staying home playing simple video games that would give their home computers viruses, as well as playing gameboy games. However, some games were complicated and a lot of reading for the young mind, so the team wanted to make a game that was easy enough for people of all ages to play and succeed in.
